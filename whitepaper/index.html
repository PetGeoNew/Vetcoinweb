VETcoin (VETC) — White paper

Abstract

VETcoin (VETC) is a native BEP-20 utility token of the PetGeo social network built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). VETcoin powers a decentralized ecosystem for pet owners, volunteers, and animal welfare organizations. The token enables payments, rewards for activity, and payments for services within the PetGeo application.

Introduction

The pet care and animal welfare market is growing rapidly, but existing solutions lack transparency, fair reward mechanisms, and true community ownership. PetGeo solves this by combining a social network with blockchain technology. VETcoin is the economic foundation of the ecosystem. It is not a fundraising tool, but a fully functional asset with real utility from day one.

Token Utility

VETcoin performs four main functions within the PetGeo ecosystem.

Rewards for Social Activity

Users earn VETC for:

  • Reporting lost or found pets
  • Adding verified geotags on different pet services
  • Posting and validating reliable content
  • Completing community quests and tasks

Payment for Services

Pet service providers (grooming, boarding, walking, veterinary clinics) pay VETC to post and promote their listings.

Donations and Charity

Users can send VETC directly to verified shelters, volunteer groups, or other users. All donations are transparent and recorded on the blockchain.

Access and Bonuses

VETC holders gain access to exclusive features, bonus rewards, and future ecosystem drops.
